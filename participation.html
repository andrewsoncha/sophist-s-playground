How to Participate

Sophist's Playground 2021!

The statement for participants to prove for Sophist's Playground 2021 is: 7x6=39.

Try to prove the above statement in the most creative way you can think of! You are free to use any theorems or definitions or concepts from algebra, geometry, statistics, logic, science, computer science, etc. We highly recommend you read the contest rules and example proofs before submitting.

Application Deadline

The application deadline for your proofs is until November 1st, 2021! The winner will be announced in November, 2021.

How to Participate

Send an email to andrewsoncha2@gmail.com with the proof. The proof has to be in the form of a word file or an image file of the proof written on paper.

What you need to include in your submission

  • Name(It doesn't matter if it's a username or a real name)
  • In what form you want your prize in case you win(Amazon gift card or Steam gift card)
  • The proof file
